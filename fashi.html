Fashi for Food Delivery

Web Application

Project details

Description
Fashi is a web application project aims to serve people from drink store to restaurant by ordering them online and delivering them to the customer according to their location we serve to. When customer visit our page and he will see variety types of food under many categories and he can use search or filtering to specific category to find what he wants to order easily. If user doesn't have account in our service, he can so easily create new account for him and then he can order the food and also write the address that he wants to deliver to. In addition, the restaurant has their own site to receive customers’ orders and on that he can make action and cooking the food for customers. This site called Fashi Dashboard and it has many pages to perform the work easily, take feedback from customers for future improvement and to manage main site setting like phone and email. Fashi Dashboard has specialized pages for adding and deleting editing categories and foods menu. Also, they have a page to adding new users and admins.
